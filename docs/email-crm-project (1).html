Tenant Email Notification System

Replacing a fragile, manual Outlook-based distribution list with a fully automated CRM pipeline powered by HubSpot — improving deliverability, personalization, and opt-in compliance.

✅ Self-updating contact lists
📊 Open & click-rate analytics
🔒 One-click unsubscribe
📱 Mobile-friendly emails

Project Overview

A commercial property management office previously relied on a fully manual email workflow: contacts were individually entered into Outlook folders based on tenant self-submitted forms, with no automated way to update, remove, or segment contacts. This project replaced that system end-to-end with a HubSpot CRM, delivering a dynamic, compliant, and analytics-ready email notification platform.

⚠️

The Problem

  • Static contact lists required constant manual upkeep
  • No automated bounce-back removal
  • Opt-outs required emailing staff directly
  • General and sensitive notifications shared the same list
  • No open/click tracking or delivery analytics
✅

The Solution

  • HubSpot CRM with dynamic, self-updating contact lists
  • Custom email sign-up form with automatic contact creation
  • Resubmission auto-updates existing contacts
  • Personalization tokens (e.g., tenant name in greeting)
  • Built-in unsubscribe, mobile-optimized templates

The Challenge: A Broken Manual System

Managing tenant communications for a commercial property campus should be straightforward — but the legacy process created significant operational overhead and compliance risk.

How it worked before:

  1. A tenant submitted a paper or digital form with their contact information
  2. Staff manually typed each contact into a named Outlook folder (one per building)
  3. Contacts stayed in that folder indefinitely unless manually moved
  4. Bounce-backs required staff to notice and remove addresses by hand
  5. Opt-out requests arrived via individual emails to the office

This approach did not scale, produced stale lists, and offered no visibility into whether emails were actually being read.

The Solution: HubSpot CRM Pipeline

1

Data Migration

Existing Outlook contacts exported to .xls and bulk-imported into HubSpot, preserving all prior tenant records.

→
2

Sign-up Form

A HubSpot-hosted form published via QR code and short link (Bitly) captures new subscribers automatically.

→
3

Dynamic Lists

Contacts are searchable and filterable. Form submissions auto-create or auto-update records — no manual entry.

→
4

Email Campaigns

One-time blasts or automated sequences with personalization tokens, branded templates, and compliance-ready unsubscribe links.

→
5

Analytics

Delivery, open rate, and click-through rate tracked per campaign — closing the loop on what actually reaches tenants.

Key Feature Highlights

Dynamic Contact Management HubSpot’s list engine means contacts are always current. Resubmitting the sign-up form automatically updates an existing record rather than creating a duplicate — solving the stale-data problem that plagued Outlook folders.

Compliant Opt-Out Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe footer, meeting CAN-SPAM requirements without requiring tenants to contact the office.

Personalization Tokens Email greetings auto-populate with the tenant’s name, making mass communications feel more personal and increasing engagement.

Delivery Analytics The HubSpot dashboard tracks delivery status, open rates, and click-through rates per campaign, giving the team concrete feedback on communication effectiveness.

Campus-Wide Rollout The launch used a printed flyer with QR code, a short link for digital sharing, and an introductory email to the existing list — all inviting tenants to opt in to the new system.

Outcomes & Impact

⏱️

Time Saved

Eliminated manual contact entry and bounce-back monitoring — routine list maintenance now requires no staff intervention.

📈

Better Reach

The QR-code and short-link sign-up flow makes it easy to grow the list beyond the original tenant base to campus employees and visitors.

🔍

Visibility

For the first time, the team can see open rates, click rates, and delivery stats — enabling data-driven improvements to future campaigns.

⚖️

Compliance

Built-in unsubscribe links and opt-in forms align the system with email marketing best practices and CAN-SPAM requirements.
